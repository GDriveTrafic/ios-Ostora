Managing Your Privacy Settings

FUTEMAX respects your privacy and provides several ways to control your data and ad preferences:

Ad Preferences

To manage personalized ads in the app:

  1. Tap the menu icon in the top corner
  2. Select "Settings"
  3. Go to "Privacy & Ads"
  4. Toggle "Personalized Ads" on or off

Optional Permissions

The app may request these optional permissions which you can grant or deny:

Permission Usage How to Control
Storage For caching match data and images Device Settings → Apps → FUTEMAX → Permissions

Clearing Your Data

To clear cached data in the app:

  1. Go to "Settings"
  2. Select "Storage"
  3. Tap "Clear Cache"

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I use the custom stream player?

A: Simply enter your M3U8 URL in the designated field within the app to stream personalized content. The app will verify the URL format before attempting to play the content.

Q: Is the app available for immediate use?

A: Yes! No account creation is required. Download the app and start using it right away.

Q: What data sources are used for live scores and news?

A: All data is pulled from public sports APIs and databases that provide real-time match information.

Q: Does the app use my location?

A: No, FUTEMAX does not request or require access to your device's location. We may use your general region (based on IP address) to provide relevant content, but this doesn't require any special permissions.

Q: How do I reduce the frequency of ads?

A: You can adjust ad settings in the app's "Settings" menu under "Privacy & Ads." We strive to maintain a balance between free content and user experience.

Q: Is my streaming content private?

A: Yes, the content you stream via custom M3U8 URLs is processed locally on your device and is not monitored or recorded by our servers.

Troubleshooting

App Performance Issues

  • App crashes or freezes: Try closing other apps running in the background, restart your device, or reinstall the app.
  • Slow loading times: Check your internet connection. The app requires a stable connection for optimal performance.
  • Black screen when streaming: Verify that your M3U8 URL is valid and the content is accessible from your location.

Content Issues

  • Match data not updating: Pull down to refresh the screen or check your internet connection.
  • Missing matches: Some matches may not be available due to regional restrictions or data provider limitations.

If you encounter persistent issues not addressed here, please contact our support team with details about your device model and operating system version.
