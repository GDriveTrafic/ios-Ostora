Key Features of Live Score Soccer

  • Live match scores: Track accurate and up-to-date match progress in real-time.
  • Match info: Get detailed match stats, including team lineups, player performance, and more.
  • Custom stream player: Enter your M3U8 URL to stream personalized content directly to your device.
  • Sports News: Get the latest soccer news, including transfer news, injury updates, league standings, and more.
  • Simple interface: Intuitive and clean design makes it easy for anyone to use.
  • No signup required: Get started immediately without creating an account.
  • Free to use: Completely free to use with no subscription fees or hidden charges.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I use the custom stream player?

A: Simply enter your M3U8 URL in the designated field within the app to stream personalized content.

Q: Is the app available for immediate use?

A: Yes! No account creation is required. Download the app and start using it right away.

Q: What data sources are used for live scores and news?

A: All data is pulled from public sports APIs and databases.

Privacy and Security

Zero data collection: Your privacy is our top priority. We do not track or collect any personal information.

Local storage: All your playlists and settings are securely stored on your device, giving you complete control over your data.

Independently developed app: Built in-house with no external dependencies or third-party services, ensuring maximum security and reliability.
