Your Privacy Choices

Manage your data preferences and rights in the FUTEMAX app

1. Personalized Advertising

For iOS Users

App Tracking Transparency: When you first launch the app, you'll receive a prompt asking permission to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites. You can:

  • Select "Allow" to receive personalized ads
  • Select "Ask App Not to Track" to opt out of personalized ads

Change your choice later: Go to your device's Settings → Privacy → Tracking, find FUTEMAX in the list, and toggle the permission.

Additional iOS ad controls: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → toggle "Personalized Ads"

For Android Users

Control personalized ads through:

  • In-app settings: Open FUTEMAX → Menu → Settings → Privacy & Ads → toggle "Personalized Ads"
  • Device settings: Go to Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of personalized advertising

Note: Opting out of personalized ads doesn't mean you'll see fewer ads, just that they won't be tailored to your interests.

2. Optional Location Permission

FUTEMAX can use your approximate location to provide regional content and improve the relevance of information you see. This permission is completely optional and the app works fully without it.

For iOS Users

Manage location permissions:

  • Settings → Privacy → Location Services → FUTEMAX
  • Choose between: "Never", "Ask Next Time", "While Using the App"

For Android Users

Manage location permissions:

  • Settings → Apps → FUTEMAX → Permissions → Location
  • Choose appropriate permission level

3. Ad Network Privacy Controls

Our app uses multiple advertising networks, each with their own privacy controls:

  • Google AdMob: Google Ad Settings
  • Facebook Audience Network: Facebook Privacy Settings
  • AppLovin: AppLovin Privacy Settings

For mobile-specific opt-outs, you can also use:

  • iOS: Limited Ad Tracking (Settings → Privacy → Tracking)
  • Android: Opt out of Ads Personalization (Settings → Google → Ads)

4. Data Deletion Requests

You can request deletion of any personal data we hold about you:

  • Email adamjhon4444@gmail.com with subject "Data Deletion Request"
  • Specify what data you want deleted
  • Include information that helps us identify you (such as device type and app installation date)

We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Clear local app data: You can also clear locally stored data directly in the app via Settings → Storage → Clear Cache, or by uninstalling the app.

5. Children's Privacy

The FUTEMAX app is not directed at children under 13 years of age. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at adamjhon4444@gmail.com and we will promptly remove such information.

For parents: If you believe your child is using our app and is under 13, you can contact us to:

  • Review information we've collected from your child
  • Request deletion of that information
  • Prevent further collection or use of your child's information

6. Your Legal Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have specific legal rights:

California Residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion of your data, opt-out of the sale of your data, and non-discrimination for exercising these rights.

European Users (GDPR): You have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, object to processing, and data portability.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at adamjhon4444@gmail.com.
